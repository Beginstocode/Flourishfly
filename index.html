What is a static website?

+

A static website is one that delivers pre-built content to the user's web browser. It's generally faster, more secure, and less complex to host, making it perfect for businesses that primarily need an online brochure or information hub.

How long does it take to build a website?

+

Our delivery times vary by plan: Starter (3 Days), Growth (5-7 Days), and Flourish Pro (7-10 Days). These times are from when we have all necessary content from you.

Do you provide website hosting?

+

Our pricing plans primarily cover design and development. We can guide you on reliable and affordable hosting providers, and even help with initial setup, but hosting costs are separate.

What is SEO and why do I need it?

+

SEO (Search Engine Optimization) helps your website rank higher on Google and other search engines. This means more potential customers find your business organically, increasing traffic and leads without paid ads.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

+

Yes, absolutely! We understand businesses grow. You can upgrade from Starter to Growth or Growth to Flourish Pro at any time. We'll adjust the pricing based on the difference between the plans.
